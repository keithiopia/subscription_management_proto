GOV.UK Notification discovery and sign up prototypes

First round of research is of the subscription discovery and sign up flow. Trying to give an idea of potential notification frequency for new and existing users, without requiring an login.

Prototype asks for email address. If you're an existing user, it will show you a total quantity of notifications for your account, but not the details of what the user is already subscribed to.

3. existing-user-4.html
as an existing user, sign up to get a daily email for updates to a large topic (refine down to only get GCSE content) confirmation link from email
